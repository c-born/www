BATTERY MANUFACTURE

C-Born provided design work for battery manufacturing machinery. Allen Bradley PLC-2s were used in the designs, which included control panels and control circuits.

Systems included:

Design of battery baking oven using Eurotherm Thyristor units
Design of battery vent tester for locating battery vents, with pressure testing implemented using a Qualitek vacuum tester
Design of battery acid filler that locates batteries for filling and provides various acid filling functions
